About Me

My hockey team is the Tampa Bay Lightning, and I try to play hockey twice a week. I am still new to the coding world (around 4 months), but I've found myself becoming more and more excited to learn about programming. I'm always eager to pick up new tools in my coding arsenal.

I am a very creative person. Its fun to inject a little of your personality into the things you create. In my expierence so far I've found coding to be a great way to strech my more creative muscles and I hope that I can continue to grow and eventually create bigger and better things.
